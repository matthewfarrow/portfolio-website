Tropic Vote Live on Base Flagship

Non-custodial vote optimization for Hydrex's ve(3,3) governance. Holders delegate earning power only. The veNFT never leaves their wallet and they can withdraw at any time. It votes and claims every epoch, routing earning power to the pools with the best marginal expected value. Holders keep 95%, the protocol takes 5%. I do the product, the frontend and the partner side. I do not write the contracts or run the keeper.

+41% vs network average reward per vote, epoch 42.  ·  +50% all-time efficiency vs the average voter, rewards-weighted, reconciled on-chain across every closed epoch.  ·  $972 rewards captured all-time.

Tropic Vote is mine, not Hydrex's. I work in partnerships at Hydrex and I built this on Hydrex's ve(3,3) governance, so the 5% accrues to Tropic Vote and not to my employer. Say that out loud before quoting any of it. The +41% and the +50% are two different metrics over two different windows and I am not going to blend them into one flattering number. And the $972 is small: nine and a half million earning power delegated against $972 captured all-time is what an early protocol looks like. The efficiency is the claim, the volume is not. These figures move every epoch, so do not quote them as constants.
